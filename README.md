## 🚀 Professional Profile

I build and sustain mission-critical AI infrastructure. Bridging the gap between academic research and engineering reality, I focus on scalable architecture, continuous observability, and mathematically grounded operations.

- 🚄 **Current Focus:** MLOps & Cloud Specialist at **Rumo Logística**, architecting the automated infrastructure for the Operations Control Center (CCO) managing 14,000+ km of railways.
- 🔬 **Research:** PhD Candidate at **UFPA / LASSE** (in partnership with Ericsson), researching multivariate **Concept Drift** detection for noisy, real-time data streams in 5G/6G networks.
- ⚙️ **Core Philosophy:** I prioritize metrics, system design, and building robust pipelines that ensure high availability and reliability in production environments.
